# Hybrid Information

Hybrid digimon flex between damage and utility. This guide covers hybrid skills.

---

**Skills are displayed MAX level and pending beta changes

### Tank Stance

![Skill 1](images/guides/hybrid/hybrid1.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Decreases damage taken by 40%, increases Max HP by 20000, Defense by 3000 for 300 seconds

### Melee Stance

![Skill 2](images/guides/hybrid/hybrid2.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Increases Attack Speed by 3%, Attack by 30%, Critical Damage by 100% for 300 seconds

### Ranged Stance

![Skill 3](images/guides/hybrid/hybrid3.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Increases Attack Speed by 3%, Attack Range to 12000, Critical Damage by 100% for 300 seconds

### Caster Stance

![Skill 4](images/guides/hybrid/hybrid4.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Increases Skill Damage by 100%, Intelligence by 150%, MAX DS By 20000 for 300 seconds

### Lesser Restoration

![Skill 5](images/guides/hybrid/hybrid5.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Heals party members by 8%, increases Intelligence by 150%, MAX DS By 20000 for 300 seconds
