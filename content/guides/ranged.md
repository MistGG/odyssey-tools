# Ranged Information

Ranged digimon attack from a distance. This guide covers ranged skills.

---

**Skills are displayed MAX level and pending beta changes

- **Info:** Increases Attack Speed by 3%, Attack by 30%, Critical Damage by 100% for 15 seconds
