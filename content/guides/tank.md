# Tank Information

Tanks absorb damage and protect the party. This guide covers tank skills.

---

**Skills are displayed MAX level and pending beta changes

### Attack Break

![Attack Break](images/guides/tank/tank1.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Decreaes Target's Attack by 15% for 30 seconds

### Iron Will

![TIron Will](images/guides/tank/tank2.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Decreases damage taken by 40% for 15 seconds

### Sprint

![Sprint](images/guides/tank/tank3.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **Info:** Increase move speed by 2000%

### Shield Wall

![Shield Wall](images/guides/tank/tank4.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Gives a damage shield equivalent of 20% of current HP to party members

### Digizoid Skin

![Digizoid Skin](images/guides/tank/tank5.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Reflects 5% of the Damage Taken
