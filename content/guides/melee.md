# Melee Information

Melee digimon deal close-range damage. This guide covers melee skills.

---

**Skills are displayed MAX level and pending beta changes

### Berserker Soul

![Skill 1](images/guides/melee/melee1.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Increases Attack Speed by 3%, Attack by 30%, Critical Damage by 100% for 15 seconds

### Inner Energy

![Skill 2](images/guides/melee/melee2.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Heals self HP by 10%

### Sprint

![Sprint](images/guides/melee/melee3.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **Info:** Increase move speed by 2000%

### Ultimate Instinct

![Skill 4](images/guides/melee/melee4.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Inceases Evasion by 1000% for 5 seconds

### Digital Hazard

![Skill 5](images/guides/melee/melee5.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Inceases Attack Speed by 5%, Attack by 300%, Critical Damage by 500% for 10 seconds
