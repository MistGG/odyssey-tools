# Support Information

Support digimon heal and buff allies. This guide covers support skills.

---

**Skills are displayed MAX level and pending beta changes

### Magia Code: Restoration

![Magia Code: Restoration](images/guides/support/support1.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Heals Party members by 50%

### Magia Code: Cleanse

![Magia Code: Cleanse](images/guides/support/support2.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Removes all debuffs from your party members

### Sprint

![Sprint](images/guides/support/support3.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **Info:** Increase move speed by 2000%

### Magia Code: Protection

![Magia Code: Protection](images/guides/support/support4.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Increases Damage reduction by 99% for 5 seconds for all party members

### Magia Code: Alpha

![Magia Code: Alpha](images/guides/support/support5.png)

- **DS:** 1
- **CD:** 5 second
- **Info:** Increases Intellgence by 150%, MAX DS By 20000 for 30 seconds
