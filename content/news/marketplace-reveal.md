DM:Odyssey has a unique custom marketplace. Gone are the days of browsing bazaars, using Discord Bots and the official market place.

In its place is a proper marketplace that allows you to buy/sell items. You can also see market statistics, with a full guide on the market place in-game. Additionally, a sneak peek of the CROWN item was visible in the market place making it far easier to buy/sell crowns.
