This server supports a ton of QoL improvements, here's a few!

- Auto-Cloning
- Walk around by just clicking on the map
- Hide all players (with filters for friends, party and guild!)
- Raid Teleport immediately to any raid from anywhere to anywhere!
- Loot tables are now visible on the map per digimon!
- Mob Find on the map lets you travel straight to a digimon, it'll even port you there!
- Gear comparison right in the UI by hovering over gear
